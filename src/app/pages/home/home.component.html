The Three S's of Bullying

Share

If you ever feel that you are being bullied at school , the first step you must take is to share the instance/s with a person you trust. This person can be any of your teachers or school counsellor.

Stop

You as a by-stander of a bullying situation,should intervene and try to handle the situation by stopping the bully. If you feel that is not in your capacity, you must report it to any school authority in your nearest vicinity.

Spread

The most important step is to spread awareness about bullying and sensitising everyone not to indulge in such practices.
